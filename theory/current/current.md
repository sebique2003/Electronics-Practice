# Current

## EN

Current is the flow of electric charge through a conductor.

Unit: Ampere (A)

Example:
- phone charger: 2A
- LED circuit: ~20mA


## RO

Curentul electric este fluxul de sarcină electrică printr-un conductor.

Unitate: Amper (A)

Exemple:
- încărcător telefon: 2A
- circuit LED: ~20mA
